Common Phrases to Get Around

Travelling on a budget can be hard and often public transport is our only option. Below are some phrases that can help you both find your way to an attraction, a mode of transport or home. Combine these with the vocabulary above in order to string together a rough sentence. Feel free to use the translator below to assist.

  • How can I get to (certain attraction or place)? - Come posso arrivare a (certain attraction or place)?
  • Where is the nearest train station? - dove si trova la stazione ferroviaria?
  • Where is the nearest bus stop? - Dove si trova la fermata dell'autobus più vicina?

Common Phrases to Find Food

Here are a few phrases that will help you finded the nearest restaurant, as well as order

  • How much does this cost? - Quanto costa questo?
  • Does this have (ingredient)? - Questo ha (ingredient)?
  • Where is the nearest restaurant? - Dove si trova il ristorante più vicino?
