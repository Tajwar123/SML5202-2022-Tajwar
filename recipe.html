Ingredients

  • 6 breaded fish fillets
  • 12 taco shells
  • Seasoning Mix
  • 600g coleslaw

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven as instructed on pack for fish fillets. Cook fish fillets to pack instructions, then cut each fillet into bite size pieces.
  2. Heat the taco shells to pack instructions
  3. Mix the mayonnaise and seasoning mix together in a large bowl and stir in the coleslaw. Fill the taco shells with the fish and coleslaw and serve
