Places to Visit

Italy is a wonderful country with many things to see. From grand cities teeming with history and culture, to picturesque coastlines, raging mountains and serene lakes, one could spend a lifetime exploring this country. However as a traveller, especicially one with a budget, I understand that its simply not feasible, both cost and time-wise, to see everything Italy has to offer. So taking from my experiences, below are the must-visit cities and sights of Italy that will give you a well-rounded appreciation of culture and life there, without breaking the bank.
